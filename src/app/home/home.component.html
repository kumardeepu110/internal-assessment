We provide the Online Internal Assessment

At the Diploma program stage, the students are required to study the various subject groups at a higher level and lower level. The internal assessments are mandatory and are conducted in addition to the Extended Essay (EE) and Theory of Knowledge (TOK). Both these levels require the students to complete two types of assessments, which are:

Internal Assessment
External Assessment

Students are required to prepare differently for each Internal Assessment (IA) as they differ in requirements. Internal assessments are graded by the school teacher and moderated by an IB examiner. Hence, Students must take the help of expert tutors, who have rich experience in writing Internal Assessments.
